About / Contact

At HSWAR, the focus is on discovering and recommending websites and apps, primarily those that are open-source and privacy-focused. Additionally, unique Android home screen setups, custom widgets, and original wallpapers created by the HSWAR team are shared.

When sharing on any platform, please remember to credit the work, as this helps acknowledge the effort involved and supports the growth of HSWAR.

If you have any feedback, suggestions for improvements, or specific requests, please feel free to reach out using the contact information at the end of the page. Your input is valuable to us!
