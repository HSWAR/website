Sharing Past Android Homescreen Setups

These Android homescreen setups, created between 2020 and 2022, showcase various designs that may include icon packs and widgets not available in the Google Play Store. For any unavailable icons, you can create or find similar ones using Icon Pack Studio. These setups are intended to inspire and provide ideas for customizing your own home screen. If you have any specific requests or suggestions, please send an email; you can find the contact information on the About/Contact page.
