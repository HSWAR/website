Must-Try Music Apps: My Top Picks

Photo by Filip on Unsplash

When it comes to music streaming, there are numerous options available, each with its unique features and benefits. In this article, we will explore three music apps that offer a range of features, from ad-free listening to offline playback and customization options. Whether you're a Spotify fan or a YouTube Music enthusiast, these apps are sure to enhance your music streaming experience.

1. Spotube

Platforms: Android | iOS | Windows | Mac | Linux

Developed by Kingkor Roy Tirtho, Spotube is a Spotify client that lets you log in and access your playlists instantly, with premium features like:

  • No ads: Enjoy uninterrupted music.
  • Freely downloadable tracks: Save your favorite songs for offline listening.
  • Unlimited song skips: Skip any song without restrictions.
  • Cross-Device Control: Manage playback across multiple devices.

It also offers:

  • Cross-platform support
  • Anonymous/guest login
  • Time-synced lyrics
  • Native performance
  • Native performance with no telemetry or user data collection
  • Playback control is done locally, not on the server
  • Android Auto Support

Spotube uses Spotify's data API and alternative audio sources like YouTube, Piped.video, or JioSaavn, eliminating the need for Spotify Premium.

Limitations

Spotify Shows and Podcasts are not supported, as they require a premium subscription. Additionally, Spotube does not support Spotify Listen Along.

2. Harmony Music

Platforms: Android | Windows | Linux

Developed by GitHub user anandnet, Harmony Music plays songs from YouTube and YouTube Music, offering a range of features:

  • No Ads: Enjoy music without interruptions.
  • Use Without Login: Access music without needing to create an account.
  • Sync Playlists Using Piped: Easily manage your playlists by loging into your piped account.
  • Background Music: Listen while using other apps.
  • Import Songs, Playlists, Albums, and Artists: Share directly from YouTube/YouTube Music.
  • Playlist Creation & Bookmark Support: Organize your favorite tracks.
  • Song Cache for Offline Play: Listen without an internet connection.
  • Skip Silence: Automatically skip silent sections of songs.
  • Equalizer: Customize your sound experience.
  • Lyrics Support: View lyrics while listening.
  • Android Auto Support: Use the app seamlessly in your car.

3. YouTube Music (th-ch)

Platforms: Mac | Windows | Linux

A desktop-only app that provides a native look and feel for YouTube Music on your computer. It's an Electron wrapper around YouTube Music that allows customization through plugins. Key features include:

  • Ad Blocker: Blocks all ads and tracking.
  • Album Actions: Easily manage likes and dislikes for songs in playlists.
  • Dynamic Album Color Theme: Changes the app's theme based on the album's color palette.
  • Ambient Mode: Creates a lighting effect based on the video.
  • Audio Compressor: Balances audio levels for a better listening experience.
  • Bypass Age Restrictions: Access content without age verification.
  • Downloader: Download MP3s directly from the interface.
  • Synced Lyrics: Provides lyrics in sync with the music.
  • Picture-in-Picture Mode: Allows you to watch videos while using other apps.
  • Custom Shortcuts: Set global hotkeys for playback control.

In conclusion, these three music apps offer a unique set of features that can enhance your music streaming experience. Whether you're looking for ad-free listening, offline playback, or customization options, there's an app on this list that's sure to meet your needs. With their user-friendly interfaces and range of features, Spotube, Harmony Music, and YouTube Music (th-ch) are definitely worth checking out. Also recommended to support the artists by engaging with their content or by buying their media, merch, or concert tickets.
